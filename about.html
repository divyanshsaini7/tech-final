Welcome to Sunrise Public School, a leading institution dedicated to academic excellence and holistic growth. Established in 2005, our school has continuously strived to create an environment where students not only learn but thrive.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for nurturing creativity, critical thinking, and moral values. Our teachers are dedicated professionals who ensure every student receives personal attention and encouragement.

Vision: To empower young minds to achieve excellence through education, integrity, and innovation.

Mission: To provide quality education and prepare students to face global challenges with confidence and compassion.

The institution emphasizes a balance between academics, sports, arts, and co-curricular activities, ensuring the all-round development of every learner.

Our campus is equipped with modern facilities, digital classrooms, and a student-centered Student Information System (SIS) to connect parents, teachers, and administration seamlessly.
